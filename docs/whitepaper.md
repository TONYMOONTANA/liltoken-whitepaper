# $LILTOKEN: "Say Hello to My Lil Token!"

## Executive Summary
_"The world is yours."_

$LILTOKEN ($LIL) is a decentralized meme coin inspired by the legendary film Scarface. Our mission: **"To create a decentralized, community-driven token that leverages the enduring appeal of Scarface to foster engagement, utility, and long-term value in the crypto space."** From the bold contract address drop to the blimp soaring above Miami, $LILTOKEN brings together film nostalgia and crypto innovation.

## Token Economics (Tokenomics)
**Token Name**: $LILTOKEN  
**Symbol**: $LIL  
**Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 $LIL

**Distribution**:  
- 50% Community  
- 20% Team (with vesting)  
- 15% Marketing  
- 15% Liquidity pools

**Vesting Schedule**:  
Team tokens unlock over 18 months to guarantee commitment and prevent dump risk.

**Utility**:  
- *Governance*: Community votes on upgrades  
- *Staking*: "First you get the money, then you get the power."  
- *NFT Purchases*: Exclusive Scarface-themed digital collectibles

**Burn Mechanism ("Cocaine Burn")**:  
A portion of tokens are burned with every transaction.  
_"I bury those cockroaches."_

**Staking and Rewards**:  
Long-term holders earn rewards and exclusive NFTs.
